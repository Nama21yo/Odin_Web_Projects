Stir-Fry

Ingredients

  • 1 pound chicken breast, sliced into thin strips
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon cornstarch
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 green bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 carrot, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup chicken broth
  • 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil (optional)
  • Cooked rice for serving

Steps

  1. In a bowl, mix the chicken strips with soy sauce and cornstarch. Set aside to marinate for 10 minutes.
  2. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a large wok or skillet over medium-high heat.
  3. Add the marinated chicken to the wok and stir-fry until it is no longer pink, about 5-7 minutes. Remove the chicken from the wok and set it aside.
  4. In the same wok, add the remaining 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil.
  5. Add the garlic, bell peppers, onion, broccoli, and carrot. Stir-fry until the vegetables are tender-crisp, about 5 minutes.
  6. Return the chicken to the wok and pour in the chicken broth and oyster sauce. Stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes until everything is well-coated and heated through.
  7. If desired, drizzle with sesame oil for added flavor.
  8. Serve the stir-fry over cooked rice.
