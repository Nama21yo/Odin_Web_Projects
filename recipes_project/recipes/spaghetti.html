Spaghetti

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1 (6-ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 12 ounces spaghetti
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large pot, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the pot and cook until the onion is translucent.
  3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, oregano, basil, salt, and pepper. Bring to a simmer.
  4. Add the spaghetti to the pot, breaking it in half if necessary to fit. Stir to combine.
  5. Cover the pot and cook, stirring occasionally, until the spaghetti is tender, about 10-12 minutes.
  6. Serve the spaghetti with the meat sauce, and sprinkle with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
